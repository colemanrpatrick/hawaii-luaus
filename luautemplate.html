Germaine's Luau - Oahu Luau

Held on a scenic private beach that overlooks the sunset, Germaine's Luau offers a laid-back atmosphere. It is a great luau for the whole family! The sounding of the conch shell signals the start of the evening's festivities. Witness the Royal Court of Polynesia procession and the imu ceremony, in which the kalua pua'a (roast pig) is taken out from the traditional underground oven. The luau feast features a wide variety of delicacies, from Hawaiian dishes to continental American fare. The feast is followed by an exciting Polynesian show, with music and dances from Tahiti, Samoa, Fiji, Aotearoa and Hawaii.

A vegetarian entrée option is available upon request.
